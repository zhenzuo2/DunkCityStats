Privacy Policy

Dunk City Player Stats

Effective date: March 3, 2026

Dunk City Player Stats ("the App") respects your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

The App is designed to use player/stat data for viewing and comparison.

If you choose to post ratings/comments, you must sign in with Apple. We store your Apple user identifier, display name (if available), rating, comment text, player name, and comment timestamp.

2. How Information Is Used

We use data only to provide app features, including:

  • Viewing player stats
  • Comparing players
  • Showing rankings
  • Publishing and displaying shared ratings/comments
  • Associating posted comments with your sign-in identity in app data

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Ratings/comments you submit are visible to other users in the app.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple services, including Sign in with Apple and CloudKit, to authenticate users and store shared comments.

The App does not use third-party ads or analytics SDKs.

5. Data Storage and Retention

User-generated content (ratings/comments) is stored in Apple's CloudKit and may be retained to keep community content available.

Certain sign-in/session data may also be stored locally on your device.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

You can stop all collection by uninstalling the App.

You can clear local data by deleting/reinstalling the App. Cloud comments already posted may remain visible to others.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updates will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
